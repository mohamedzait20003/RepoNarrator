id: match-voice
title: Match the developer's voice
---
When a current README exists, treat it as the developer's own voice sample.

- Mirror its register (casual vs. formal), emoji density, heading style, and use
  of tables/badges.
- Keep phrases and taglines the developer clearly chose on purpose.
- Improve clarity and fill gaps from the résumé/projects — don't overwrite their
  personality with a generic template.
