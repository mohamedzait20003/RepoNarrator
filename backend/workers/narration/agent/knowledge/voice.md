title: Voice — write like a human, not an AI
---
Write like the developer wrote it themselves, not like a marketing bot:

- First person, plain and confident. Short paragraphs, varied sentence lengths.
- Ban AI-tell clichés: "passionate about", "results-driven", "cutting-edge",
  "seamless", "leverage", "robust", "in today's fast-paced world", "as a highly
  motivated…", and empty hype adjectives. State concrete facts instead.
- Don't open with "Hi, I'm X, a passionate developer." Lead with something
  specific and real about their work.
- Match the current README's tone, emoji usage, and section conventions when one
  exists: if they use a few section emoji, keep that habit; if they use none,
  add none.
- Always prefer a specific detail from the résumé or a project over generic
  filler.
