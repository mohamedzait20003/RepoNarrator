id: match-voice
title: Match the developer's voice (not their layout)
---
When a current README exists, treat it as a sample of how the developer writes
and presents themselves.

- Mirror the register (casual vs. formal), emoji density, and phrasing. If they
  use no emoji, add none.
- Keep taglines, names for things, and wording they clearly chose on purpose.
- Preserve their real content and their embeds; fill gaps from the résumé and
  projects rather than replacing their personality with a template.
- The **layout is yours to redesign** for the target role and reader — match the
  person's *voice*, not necessarily their old section order. Your job is a better
  version of *their* README, retargeted — not a different person, and not a
  carbon copy of what they had.
